Frequently Asked Questions - Job Seekers

How do I create a professional profile?

Go to your profile settings, add your work experience, education, skills, and a professional photo. A complete profile increases your chances of getting hired.

Can I apply for multiple jobs at once?

Yes, you can apply for as many jobs as you qualify for. We recommend tailoring your application for each position.

How do I know if an employer viewed my application?

You'll receive notifications when employers view your application, shortlist you, or schedule interviews.

Frequently Asked Questions - Employers

How do I create an effective job posting?

Include clear job titles, detailed descriptions, required qualifications, and company information. Use relevant keywords to attract the right candidates.

Can I edit a job posting after publishing?

Yes, you can edit any job posting at any time. Changes will be reflected immediately to applicants.

How do I contact applicants?

Use our messaging system to communicate with applicants directly through the platform while maintaining privacy.
